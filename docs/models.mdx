Each model ID follows `{family}/{variant}`.

- **Video** prices are **per second** of output.
- **Image** prices are **per generation**.

<Note>
  Call `GET /v1/models` for the live catalog (`price_usd` + `price_unit`).
  A 5-second video at `$0.22/sec` starts at `$1.10`, but **image-to-video and longer clips are quoted live** via `POST /v1/quote` because upstream cost varies by duration, resolution, and inputs. We always charge at least WaveSpeed cost plus tiered margin (30% Seedance 2.5, 15% other video, 10% images), rounded up to the nearest cent.
</Note>
